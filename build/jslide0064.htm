Speaker

An elaborate and creative person speaking at the conference. Typically he is very busy preparing the talk - or talks. Of course he likes to see materials on the conference CD, but only has limited time to notice that there will be one - and to provide and organize it.

If slides are ready before CD burning, then probably just minutes before. It has to be easy to integrate his materials or they cannot be integrated.
